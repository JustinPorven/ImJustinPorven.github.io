Justin Andrew Porven

I am an entry-level programmer looking to make a mark in the tech industry. I have
always been fascinated by the power of technology to change the world, and programming
provides me a way to be a part of that change. From a young age, I have always been
intrigued by puzzles and problem-solving. As a child, I spent hours playing with toys and games
that challenged me to think critically and creatively to find solutions.


When I discovered programming, I found that it combined my love for problem-solving with
my interest in technology. Programming presented me with a new set of challenges to overcome,
from debugging errors to optimizing code for efficiency. I am continually inspired by the limitless
possibilities of what can be achieved through programming and the power of technology
to solve some of the world's most pressing issues.


I am currently completing my degree in computer programming and have gained experience
in various programming languages such as C++, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. While my academic
background has given me a solid foundation in programming, I am eager to learn and grow further in this field.


Thank you for taking the time to read about me. I look forward to exploring
and learning from opportunities in the programming field.
